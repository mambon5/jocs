Yuca bread - cheesy time!!!

credits: credits: @hechoxmamá youtube channel. A very traditional and experienced chef from Colombia. Find the chef here

(press anywhere to overflowwww!! :D)

basic ingredients: butter, sugar, eggs, flour
  • preheat oven at 375F or 190C
  • take: 1/2 table spoon of olive oil,
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt,
  • 2-4 table spoon of milk(dairy or vegan),
  • 230gr yuca flour,
  • 230gr cheese (mozzarella like) - queso fresco - grated cheese,
  • add 1 eggy egg
  • -->mix well together
  • -->add a table spoon of milk and mix again
  • -->add another tbl spoon of milk and mix again
  • -->get a nice moist smooth dough
  • bake for 20-25min.
  • make billiard-sized round balls add flour if dough is too liquidish!
  • conservation tips: You can keep the dough in the fridge for at least 1 day, in the freezer for at least 1 month
