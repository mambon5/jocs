Plots of 1500 possible ground hits for the Bondar rocket using the CanSat simulator. 30 ascending trajectories are computed. Then each of them is descended using 50 different wind profiles. So that each one of the initial 30 ascending trajectories yields a total of 50 possible landing sites. The reader can notices 30 clusters of 50 points each. Each cluster corresponds to a different ascending trajectory.

A 2km by 2km grid is used to allow the reader to compute the distances between groundhits and launching points.
